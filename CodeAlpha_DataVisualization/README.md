# CodeAlpha Task 2 - Data Visualization using Superstore Dataset

## Project Overview

This project was completed as part of the CodeAlpha Data Analytics Internship Program. The objective of this task is to transform raw business data into meaningful visual insights using Python data visualization libraries.

The analysis is performed on the Superstore dataset, which contains information about sales, profits, customers, products, and regional performance. Various visualizations were created to identify trends, patterns, and business insights.

---

## Dataset Information

* Dataset Name: Sample Superstore Dataset
* Total Records: 9,994
* Total Features: 21

The dataset contains information related to:

* Orders
* Customers
* Products
* Sales
* Profit
* Discounts
* Regions
* Categories and Sub-Categories

## Visualizations Created

### 1. Sales by Category

Analyzed total sales generated by each product category.

### 2. Profit by Category

Compared profitability across different product categories.

### 3. Sales by Region

Examined regional sales performance.

### 4. Monthly Sales Trend

Analyzed sales trends across different months.

### 5. Top 10 Sub-Categories by Sales

Identified the highest revenue-generating sub-categories.

### 6. Sales vs Profit

Explored the relationship between sales and profit.

### 7. Correlation Heatmap

Visualized correlations among numerical variables.

### 8. Profit Distribution

Studied the distribution of profit values across transactions.

---

## Key Insights

* Technology generated the highest sales and profit.
* The West region recorded the highest sales performance.
* Phones emerged as the top-selling sub-category.
* Sales showed fluctuations across different months.
* Higher sales do not always guarantee higher profits.
* Discounts negatively impact profitability.
* Most transactions generated small profits, while only a few contributed exceptionally high profits.
